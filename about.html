About Us

Phoenix Academy AZ provides professional training and certification preparation for restoration and remediation careers. Our programs help students build real-world skills for water damage restoration, microbial remediation, and asbestos safety compliance.

WRT

Water Remediation Technician (WRT)

Learn the fundamentals of water damage restoration and structural drying.

  • Water damage categories and classes
  • Structural drying principles
  • Moisture detection tools
  • Equipment operation (air movers, dehumidifiers)
  • Safety and contamination prevention
AMRT

Applied Microbial Remediation Technician (AMRT)

Training focused on mold and microbial contamination remediation.

  • Mold inspection and assessment
  • Containment and PPE procedures
  • Cleaning and removal techniques
  • HVAC contamination sources
  • Post-remediation verification
ASB

Asbestos Certification

Learn safe handling and compliance procedures for asbestos-containing materials.

  • Asbestos identification
  • Health risk awareness
  • Regulatory compliance standards
  • Safe removal procedures
  • Site decontamination practices
