About Us

Phoenix Academy AZ provides professional training and certification preparation for restoration and remediation careers. Our programs are designed to help students gain real-world skills needed for water damage restoration, microbial remediation, and asbestos handling compliance.

We focus on industry-recognized certifications that prepare students for entry-level and advanced fieldwork in environmental restoration services.

WRT

Water Remediation Technician

The WRT course introduces the fundamental principles of water damage restoration. Students learn how to assess, mitigate, and restore properties affected by water intrusion.

  • Water damage categories and classes
  • Structural drying principles
  • Moisture detection and monitoring
  • Equipment usage (air movers, dehumidifiers, extractors)
  • Safety procedures and contamination prevention
AMRT

Applied Microbial Remediation Technician

The AMRT course focuses on the identification and remediation of microbial growth such as mold and bacteria in indoor environments.

  • Mold contamination assessment
  • Health and safety protocols (PPE and containment)
  • Removal and cleaning procedures
  • HVAC and hidden contamination sources
  • Post-remediation verification practices
ASB

Asbestos Certification

This certification provides training on the safe handling, identification, and regulatory compliance required when working with asbestos-containing materials.

  • Asbestos identification in building materials
  • Health risks and exposure prevention
  • Regulatory guidelines and compliance standards
  • Safe removal and containment procedures
  • Site preparation and decontamination practices

Career Path

  1. WRT – Foundational water damage skills
  2. AMRT – Mold and microbial remediation
  3. Asbestos Certification – Hazardous material handling

These certifications prepare students for careers in restoration companies, environmental safety teams, and disaster recovery services.
