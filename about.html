Certification Training for Restoration & Environmental Safety Careers

About Us

Phoenix Academy AZ provides professional training and certification preparation for restoration and remediation careers. Our programs help students build real-world skills for water damage restoration, microbial remediation, asbestos safety compliance, and environmental hazard control.

WRT

Water Damage Restoration Technician (WRT)

Training in the fundamentals of water damage restoration and structural drying techniques.

  • Water damage categories and classes
  • Structural drying principles
  • Moisture detection tools
  • Equipment operation (air movers, dehumidifiers)
  • Safety and contamination prevention
AMRT

Applied Microbial Remediation Technician (AMRT)

Specialized training in mold inspection, containment, and microbial remediation practices.

  • Mold inspection and assessment
  • Containment and PPE procedures
  • Cleaning and removal techniques
  • HVAC contamination sources
  • Post-remediation verification
AHERA C/S

AHERA Contractor Supervisor

AHERA Contractor/Supervisor prepares supervisors to safely oversee asbestos abatement projects.

  • Students learn EPA regulations, worksite safety, containment procedures, PPE requirements, and project documentation to ensure regulatory compliance.
AHERA W

AHERA Worker

Learn the essential skills required to safely perform asbestos abatement work in accordance with EPA AHERA regulations.

  • This course covers asbestos hazard recognition
  • , Personal protective equipment (PPE),
  • Containment procedures
  • Safe work practices
  • Decontamination
  • Waste handling
  • Regulatory compliance
HAZWOPER

HAZWOPER 40-Hour Certification

Training for hazardous waste operations and emergency response environments.

  • Hazardous material handling
  • PPE and respiratory protection
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Site safety protocols
LEAD

EPA RRP – Renovation, Repair, and Painting

EPA certification for working safely with lead-based paint in older buildings.

  • Lead-safe work practices
  • Containment procedures
  • Dust control methods
  • EPA compliance standards
AHERA

EPA AHERA Building Inspector Certification

Training for identifying asbestos hazards in schools and regulated buildings.

  • Asbestos building inspections
  • Sample collection procedures
  • AHERA regulatory compliance
  • Reporting and documentation
ICRA

ICRA 2.0 (PCRA/ICRA) Training

Infection control and risk assessment training for healthcare construction environments.

  • Infection control barriers
  • Risk assessment procedures
  • Healthcare facility compliance
  • Contamination prevention protocols
