Phoenix Academy AZ

Certification Training for Restoration & Environmental Safety Careers

About Us

The Phoenix Academy AZ provides professional training and certification preparation for restoration and remediation careers. Our programs help students build real-world skills for water damage restoration, microbial remediation, asbestos safety compliance, and environmental hazard control.

WRT

Water Damage Restoration Technician (WRT)

Training in the fundamentals of water damage restoration and structural drying techniques.

  • Water damage categories and classes
  • Structural drying principles
  • Moisture detection tools
  • Equipment operation (air movers, dehumidifiers)
  • Safety and contamination prevention
AMRT

Applied Microbial Remediation Technician (AMRT)

Specialized training in mold inspection, containment, and microbial remediation practices.

  • Mold inspection and assessment
  • Containment and PPE procedures
  • Cleaning and removal techniques
  • HVAC contamination sources
  • Post-remediation verification
AHERA C/S

AHERA Contractor Supervisor

AHERA Contractor/Supervisor prepares supervisors to safely oversee asbestos abatement projects.

  • Students learn EPA regulations, worksite safety, containment procedures, PPE requirements, and project documentation to ensure regulatory compliance.
AHERA W

AHERA Worker

Learn the essential skills required to safely perform asbestos abatement work in accordance with EPA AHERA regulations.

  • This course covers asbestos hazard recognition
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Containment procedures
  • Safe work practices
  • Decontamination
  • Waste handling
  • Regulatory compliance
AHERA BI

AHERA Building Inspector Certification Training

Learn how to inspect buildings for asbestos-containing materials in accordance with EPA AHERA regulations. This certification training prepares professionals for asbestos inspection and compliance work in schools and commercial buildings.

  • Inspection procedures documentation
  • Bulk sampling techniques
  • Hazard assessment
  • Regulatory compliance for schools and other regulated facilities
  • Documentation
AHERA MP

AHERA Management Planner

Learn how to develop and implement asbestos management plans in accordance with EPA AHERA regulations.

  • Risk assessment long-term management of asbestos-containing materials in schools and other regulated facilities.
  • Response action recommendations
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Long-term management of asbestos-containing materials in schools and other regulated facilities
  • Documentation
AHERA PD

AHERA Project Designer

AHERA Project Designer prepares students to design compliant asbestos abatement projects, develop technical specifications, recommend engineering controls, and ensure EPA AHERA regulatory compliance.

  • Asbestos abatement project design
  • Engineering controls and containment
  • Project specifications and planning
  • Air monitoring considerations
  • Regulatory compliance
AHERA 2HA

AHERA 2-Hour Asbestos Awareness Training Certification

>

AHERA 2-Hour Asbestos Awareness training provides essential certification for workers who may encounter asbestos during their job duties, including hazard recognition and EPA compliance requirements.

  • Asbestos identification and hazard recognition
  • Health effects of asbestos exposure
  • Safe work practices
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) awareness
  • Emergency procedures and reporting
  • EPA AHERA awareness requirements
OSHA 10/30

OSHA 10/30-Hour Construction Safety Certification Training

OSHA 10/30-hour construction safety training covers essential workplace hazard recognition, fall protection, electrical safety, and OSHA compliance for construction and industrial workers.

  • OSHA regulations and standards
  • Fall protection and prevention
  • Electrical safety
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Hazard communication
  • Job site hazard recognition
  • Worker rights and employer responsibilities
Hazwoper 40

40-Hour HAZWOPER Certification Training

The 40-hour HAZWOPER certification prepares workers for hazardous waste operations and emergency response environments, including chemical safety, PPE, decontamination, and OSHA regulatory compliance.

  • Hazardous material identification
  • Chemical and toxic substance safety
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Site characterization and hazard assessment
  • Decontamination procedures
  • Air monitoring and respiratory protectio
  • OSHA HAZWOPER regulations
  • Safe handling of hazardous waste
PCRA/ICRA

ICRA 2.0 (PCRA/ICRA)

ICRA 2.0 (PCRA/ICRA) prepares contractors, facility managers, and healthcare professionals to minimize infection risks during construction, renovation, and maintenance projects in occupied healthcare facilities.

  • Infection Control Risk Assessment (ICRA)
  • Patient Care Risk Assessment (PCRA)
  • Containment barriers and negative air systems
  • Dust and contaminant control
  • Protection of patients, staff, and visitors
  • Healthcare construction best practices
  • Documentation and communication
EPA RRP

EPA RRP – Renovation, Repair and Paint

EPA RRP – Renovation, Repair and Painting Certification prepares contractors and maintenance professionals to safely perform renovation work that may disturb lead-based paint while complying with EPA lead-safe work practice requirements.

  • Lead-based paint hazard recognition
  • EPA lead-safe work practices
  • Containment and dust control
  • EPA regulatory compliance
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
