About Us

At The Phoenix Academy, we offer comprehensive Restoration and Industrial Hygiene Classes designed to promote safety and health in the workplace. Our courses cover essential topics such as recognizing workplace hazards, assessing exposure risks, chemical safety, and understanding regulatory standards. Whether you are just starting out or looking to expand your expertise, our classes provide hands-on training and expert guidance to help you gain practical skills and industry knowledge. By attending our programs, you'll learn how to protect workers, maintain a safe environment, and stay up-to-date with the latest industrial hygiene practices.
