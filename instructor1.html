Dr. Brandon White has Over twenty-three (23) years of experience working as an industrial hygienist in the fields of environmental microbiology, water mitigation, microbial remediation, hazardous material abatement, biotechnology, and molecular biology. Experience including sample collection, sample analysis, creation of IH reports and project specifications, creation of specifications and remediation protocol for Category 1-3 water loss projects and federally regulated abatement projects involving hazardous materials. In depth experience with Homeland Security projects, administrative operations, management of field industrial hygienists, quality assurance, quality control, training, education, and safety and health oversight. Over fifteen (15) years of experience teaching biology, microbiology and pathophysiology at multiple universities and community colleges in Arizona including Maricopa Community College District. Twelve (12) years experience as an expert witness involving litigation in microbiological and regulated hazardous material cases. Ten (10) years experience teaching EPA Lead and AHERA Asbestos federal certification courses. Independent appraiser, court appointed Umpire, OSHA Outreach certified trainer through the University of San Diego, Florida state licensed education provider, and an IICRC approved instructor.
