BRANDON L. WHITE

Dr. Brandon White has over twenty-three (23) years of experience working as an industrial hygienist in the fields of environmental microbiology, water mitigation, microbial remediation, hazardous material abatement, biotechnology, and molecular biology.

Experience includes sample collection, sample analysis, creation of industrial hygiene reports and project specifications, development of remediation protocols for Category 1–3 water loss projects, and management of federally regulated hazardous material abatement projects.

Dr. White has extensive experience with Homeland Security projects, administrative operations, quality assurance, quality control, training, education, and safety and health oversight. He has more than fifteen years of teaching experience in biology, microbiology, and pathophysiology at Arizona universities and community colleges.

He has served as an expert witness in microbiological and hazardous materials litigation, taught EPA Lead and AHERA Asbestos certification courses, is an Independent Appraiser, court-appointed Umpire, OSHA Outreach trainer, and IICRC-approved instructor.
